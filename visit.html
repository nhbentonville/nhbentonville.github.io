Hello!
We know meeting someone for the first time can be intimidating, and going to a new church the first time can be nerve-racking. We want to help make your first experience at New Heights Bentonville a great one!

Service Times
We meet every Sunday and offer two identical services at 9:00 am and 10:45 am

Location + Directions
We meet at Ruth Barker Middle School in Bentonville

500 SE 18th Street
Bentonville, AR 72712

What to Expect

Service Length      

In total, New Heights Bentonville services are about 90 minutes in length. Services begin with a time of worship through music. Song lyrics are projected onto the screens so you can sing along or engage with worship however you feel most comfortable. After a time of worship, one of our teaching pastors will share an encouraging and hope-filled biblical message. We close our services with more time of worship and communion (via tables in the back).

Our Culture

Sunday's at New Heights Bentonville are casual and relaxed. We have coffee, donuts, and fruit you can grab before service. Come as you are and expect to feel welcomed as our guest. 

What About Kids?

We believe that kids should learn about Jesus and have a blast doing it every single week and at NHB Kids that is our priority. The other thing we make a priority is your children's safety. Because of that, we have a detailed check-in process for kids Infant through 6th Grade. The first time you visit you will want to leave yourself an extra 10 minutes to get checked-in. 

What About Students?

New Heights Bentonville offers special services for our students. Our 5th + 6th graders meet in the band room and our  7th - 12th graders meet in the choir room. These services are only offered at 10:45 am. 

Let us know you're coming.
Ready to check out New Heights Bentonville in person? We can't wait to meet you. Let us know when you would like to visit and we'll make sure to save a seat for you.
